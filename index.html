About Bell School

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1992

Founded by Sri. A. Chelladhurai and supported by Mrs. Lily Darling Chelladhurai, Bell School stands as an inspiring monument of learning and ethics on a sprawling six-acre campus.

We blend traditional values with modern methodologies, fostering an environment where academic curiosity is matched by strong moral character and physical vitality.

Our "Golden Triangle" partnership between faculty, students, and parents creates a nurturing ecosystem for holistic development.

Our Vision

To be a community of life-long learners demonstrating knowledge, skills, and values for global citizenship.

Our Mission

Ensuring each student achieves intellectual and personal excellence, prepared for unique career pathways.
