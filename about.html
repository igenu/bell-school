Our Legacy

Since 1992, building a foundation of character and excellence.

A Visionary Beginning

Bell Matriculation Higher Secondary School was established in 1992 by the revered philanthropist Sri. A. Chelladhurai. His vision was simple yet profound: to bring world-class education rooted in strong moral values to the heart of Tirunelveli.

Managed by the Indrani Chelladhurai Charities, the institution has grown from humble beginnings into a premier educational landmark, known for its disciplined environment and consistent academic results.
