Admission Process

Welcome to Bell School

We appreciate your interest in Bell Matriculation Higher Secondary School. We seek to admit students who will thrive in our academic environment and contribute positively to our community.

Admissions are open for the academic year 2024-2025. Seats are limited, so we encourage early application.

Age Criteria (as of June 1st)

  • Pre-KG 2+ Years
  • LKG 3+ Years
  • UKG 4+ Years
  • Class I 5+ Years

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (Original + Copy)
  • Aadhar Card Copy
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Passport Size Photos (4)
  • Previous Year Report Card
  • Community Certificate

Ready to Apply?

You can visit our admission office during working hours or contact us for more details. Application forms are available at the school office.
